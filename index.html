Ground Plane Segmentation from Unstructured Point Clouds

Tim Fratangelo & Bret Minnehan

Download .zip Download .tar.gz View on GitHub

Description

Use unstructured three dimensional point cloud data taken from an aerial vehicle to generate a mesh representing the ground plane. We plan on a multistage approach consisting of filtering, segmentation and three dimensional mesh generation. Mean filtering will be used to reduce the inherent white noise found in LIDAR data sets. Segmentation research will be focused mainly on the graph cuts approach.
