Lidar

Computational Geometry Final Project

Download .zip Download .tar.gz View on GitHub

Tim Fratangelo & Bret Minnehan

Goal

Use unstructured three dimensional point cloud data taken from an aerial vehicle to generate a mesh representing the ground plane.

Timeline

  • Week 6 - Gain a basic understanding of the PCL library, run though a few sample point cloud datasets.
  • Week 7 - Test out the builtin mesh generation, filtering and visualization features of PCL. Create a very rudimentary segmentation of the ground plane by hand.
  • Week 8 - First presentation, show before and after filtering, mesh generation and the target ground plane mesh.
  • Week 9 - Research and experiment with different segmentation techniques, focusing on graph cuts.
  • Week 10 - Implement the chosen segmentation algorithm and begin researching on the ways to determine the ground plane.
  • Week 11 - Continue researching and implementing ground plane detection.
  • Week 12 - Integration of the full pipeline
  • Week 13 - Testing/Integration, write final paper
  • Week 14 - Testing/Integration, write final paper
  • Week 15 - Final presentation
